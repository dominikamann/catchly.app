About Catchly

Catchly helps you capture every fishing adventure and fish smarter through experience. Track your catches, remember your best fishing spots, and build a comprehensive fishing journal that helps you improve with every trip.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Catchly?

Catchly is a fishing app designed to help anglers capture and remember their fishing adventures. It allows you to log your catches, save your favorite fishing spots, and track patterns to fish smarter over time.

What features does Catchly offer?

Catchly offers features including:

  • Catch logging with photos, species, size, and weight
  • Location tracking for your favorite fishing spots
  • Weather and conditions tracking
  • Personal fishing statistics and insights
  • Calendar view of your fishing trips
  • Secure cloud backup of your fishing journal

Is Catchly free to use?

Catchly offers a free version with core features. Premium features including advanced analytics, unlimited photo storage, and offline mode are available through a subscription.

What platforms is Catchly available on?

Catchly is currently available on the Apple App Store for iPhone and iPad devices running iOS 15.0 or later.

Does Catchly work offline?

Yes! You can log catches and access your existing data while offline. All changes will automatically sync when you're back online.

Can I share my catches with friends?

Absolutely! You can share individual catches or entire fishing trips with friends through social media, messaging apps, or by generating a shareable link.

How does location tracking work?

Catchly uses your device's GPS to record fishing spot locations. You can save spots with custom names and notes, making it easy to return to productive areas. Location data is stored privately and only shared if you choose to.

Is my fishing data private?

Yes! Your fishing data is private by default. You control what you share and with whom. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your information and never share your data with third parties without your consent.

Can I export my fishing data?

Yes, premium users can export their entire fishing journal in PDF or CSV format. This is perfect for keeping permanent records or analyzing your data in other tools.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account at any time from the app's Settings menu. This will permanently remove all your data from our servers. Please note that this action cannot be undone.

I found a bug or have a feature request. How can I report it?

We love hearing from our users! Please use the contact form below to report bugs or suggest new features. We read every message and use your feedback to improve Catchly.

Does Catchly support multiple languages?

Currently, Catchly is available in English. We're working on adding support for additional languages based on user demand.
