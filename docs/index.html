How do I log a catch?
Go to "Adventures", select a day, and tap "New Catch". You can add photos, species, size, weight, and location details.

How do I delete an adventure?
Select the adventure from your list, then tap "Delete" to remove it permanently.

Is my data safe?
Yes! All your data stays exclusively on your device. Catchly does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Does Catchly work offline?
Absolutely! Catchly is designed to work completely offline, perfect for remote fishing locations.
